# AarambhHEr

AarambhHEr is an AI-powered platform designed to empower women by providing career guidance, skill development resources, job opportunities, and workplace safety tools in one integrated ecosystem.

The platform helps women start or restart their professional journey with the support of AI-driven career assistance and accessible learning resources.

---

## 🌐 Live Preview

🔗 https://aarambher.netlify.app/

Explore the live web application to experience the platform's features including job discovery, AI career guidance, resume analysis, learning resources, and workplace safety tools.

## ❗ Problem Statement

Many women face barriers when entering or returning to the workforce. These challenges include:

- Limited job opportunities
- Lack of career guidance
- Skill gaps
- Workplace safety concerns

AarambhHEr aims to address these challenges through technology-driven solutions.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 📍 Location-Based Job Finder
Helps users find nearby job opportunities based on their location, skills, and preferences.

### 🤖 AI Career Advisor
An AI chatbot that provides personalized career guidance and skill recommendations.

### 📄 AI Resume Analyzer
Allows users to upload resumes and receive AI-powered feedback to improve employability.

### 🎓 Learning Hub
Provides curated YouTube learning resources to help users develop relevant skills.

### 🛡 Safety Center
A dedicated section where women can report unsafe workplaces and access emergency helplines.
